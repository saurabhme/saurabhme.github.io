Saurabh Singh

I am a Senior Research Scientist at Google, working in the field of Computer Vision and Machine Learning. I received my PhD in Computer Science from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, where I was advised by Prof. David Forsyth and Prof. Derek Hoiem. I received my MS from Robotics Institute, Carnegie Mellon University where I worked with Alyosha Efros, Abhinav Gupta and Martial Hebert.
